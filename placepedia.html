Placepedia Dataset

Size: 14TB

Placepedia dataset contains 240K places with 35M images from all over the world (click "Download All Images" to download). Each place is associated with its district, city/town/village, state/province, country, continent, and a large amount of diverse photos. Both administrative areas and places have rich side information, e.g. discription, population, category, function (click "Download Meta Data" to download). In addition, two cleaned subsets (Places-Coarse and Places-Fine) for experiments are provided. See details below.

Download All Images (Raw) (14TB) Download Meta Data (1.2G) Download Places-coarse Subset (1.7T) Download Places-fine Subset (18G)
1. Places-Coarse: There are 26K places containing Wikipedia links with 5M images. We select 200 places for validation and 400 places for testing, from 50 famous cities of 34 countries. The remained places are used for training. The images are cleaned twice for validation/testing set, but only once for training set. There are 50 categories and 10 functions. The training/validation/testing set have 5M/60K/120K images respectively.
2. Places-Fine: Places-Fine shares the same validation/testing set with Places-Coarse. For training set, we selected 400 places from the 50 cities of validation/testing places. Different from Places-Coarse, we also cleaned the training set twice. The training/validation/testing set have 110K/60K/120K images respectively, which are tagged with 50 categories, 10 functions, 50 cities, and 34 countries.
